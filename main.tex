\textbf{ICSE class 10 paper 2019}\\\\
\textbf{Q3 (b):}
$\vec{M}$ and $\vec{N}$ are two points on the X axis and Y axis respectively.
$\vec{P}(3,2)$ divides the line segment MN in the ratio 2:3.\\
Find:\\
 (i)the coordinates of $\vec{M}$ and $\vec{N}$\\
 (ii)the slope of MN.\\\\
\\\textbf{Solution:}\\
Given,\\
\hspace*{10pt}$\vec{M}$ and $\vec{N}$ are two points on X and Y axes respectively.\\
\hspace*{10pt}Let 
\begin{align}
 \vec{M} &= \myvec {a \\ 0}\\
 \vec{N} &= \myvec {0 \\ b}
\end{align}
\hspace*{10pt}$\vec{P}$ divides MN in the ratio 2:3.\\
According to Section formula,
\begin{align}
   \vec{P} &= \myvec{\dfrac{2(0)+3(a)}{2+3} \\\\ \dfrac{2(b)+3(0)}{2+3}}\\
   \vec{P} &= \myvec{\dfrac{3a}{5} \\\\ \dfrac{2b}{5}}
\end{align}
But we have,\\
  $\vec{P} = \myvec{3 \\ 2}\\ $
\begin{figure}[h]
\centering  
\includegraphics[width=\columnwidth]{figures/Figure_1.png} 
\end{figure}
Therefore,\\
 $\myvec{\dfrac{3a}{5} \\\\ \dfrac{2b}{5}}=\myvec{3 \\ 2}\\$
  $\implies$ a = 5 and b = 5\\
 
(i)$\vec{M} = \myvec{5 \\ 0}$ and $\vec{N} = \myvec{0 \\ 5}$\\\\
\hspace*{10pt}(ii) Slope of MN = $\dfrac{5-0}{0-5}$\\\\
     \hspace*{95pt} = -1
